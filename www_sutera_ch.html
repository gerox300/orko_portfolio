Core threads of my work

  • 01.(XR/MR/VR)
    Perceptual Interfaces
    Merging human and machine perception to enable seamless understanding, support, and facilitating intuitive interactions (augmented intelligence). Extending real and virtual worlds in a way that feels connected, empowering, and sparks imagination.
  • 02.
    Embodiment
    Extending or restoring humans physical abilities through HMD, exoskeletons, prosthetics, and accessibility-driven design making it truly part of you.
  • 03.
    IA & AI
    Integrating automation (AI) thoughtfully where it serves best and ensuring humans stay in the loop where their talents and judgment matter most (IA), always focusing on human autonomy and safety.
  • 04.
    System and Tools
    Designing interaction frameworks, SDKs, and human interface guidelines to democratize knowledge about design and implementation accessible to everyone.

Designing connections between humans and machines

— a living cybernetic symbiosis.🤝

I design systems that shape how humans and machines connect.

From robotic extensions to perceptual interfaces,
my work lives
at the intersection of biology and technology.

Essentially

My passion is to support living beings in developing new abilities or regaining lost abilities, while at the same time providing them with the means to achieve this for themselves or for others.

That is the meaning of “Reality by Design”: each of us can actively shape their own reality.
